_Music is very important to me - it's how a focus and relax. But my taste changes **a lot**. One day I will repeat a song over 100 times, then a week later skip it instantly when it comes on. To keep track of this, I built this app._

## Technologies used

- Next.js
- React
- Typescript
- Express.js
- Supabase
- PostgreSQL
- Cron jobs
